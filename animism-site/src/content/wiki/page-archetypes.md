Most content fits better when you pick a “shape” and stick to it. These archetypes keep pages consistent and easy to scan.

## Overview

Use for: defining a topic and pointing outward.

- Start with a short summary.
- Add key points and constraints.
- Link to deeper pages.

## How-to

Use for: tasks and repeatable workflows.

- Prerequisites
- Steps
- Examples
- Troubleshooting/pitfalls

## Reference

Use for: lists, tables, fields, and canonical details.

- Keep it structured.
- Minimize prose.
- Prefer tables where possible.

## Checklist

Use for: quality gates and “did we remember to…”.

## Glossary

Use for: short definitions and synonyms.
